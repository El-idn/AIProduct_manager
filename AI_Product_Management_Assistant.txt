PRODUCT REQUIREMENTS DOCUMENT (PRD)
Product Requirements Document (PRD)
Product Name
ProdPilot AI
Product Summary
ProdPilot AI is an AI-powered product management assistant designed for startup teams, product managers, founders, and engineering organizations. The platform automates repetitive product management workflows including PRD generation, user story creation, feature prioritization, roadmap planning, customer feedback analysis, and KPI anomaly detection.
The application combines conversational AI with structured product operations workflows in a modern collaborative dashboard.
Problem Statement
Product managers spend excessive time:
* Writing repetitive documentation
* Organizing customer feedback
* Prioritizing features manually
* Monitoring metrics across fragmented tools
* Translating ideas into engineering-ready tasks
Most existing tools either:
* Focus only on project management
* Lack AI-native workflows
* Feel overly enterprise-heavy
* Do not unify planning, analytics, and execution
Goals
* Reduce product documentation creation time by 80%
* Improve feature prioritization consistency
* Centralize AI-assisted product workflows
* Provide visually modern, real-time SaaS dashboards
* Deliver engineering-ready outputs
Target Users
Primary Users
* Product Managers
* Startup Founders
* Product Designers
* Engineering Leads
Secondary Users
* Agile Teams
* Growth Teams
* Business Analysts
